# Future Improvements

To further enhance the **Indian Food Truck Management System**, the following roadmap is proposed:

---

## Phase 4: Order & Logistics
* **Online Food Ordering**: Direct-to-truck ordering with real-time ETA.
* **Payment Integration**: Stripe/Apple Pay/Google Pay for deposits and orders.
* **SMS Notifications**: Automated Twilio alerts when a catering reply is received.

## Phase 5: Customer Retention
* **Loyalty Program**: Digital punch-cards or points-based rewards.
* **Customer Accounts**: Saved order history and preferred catering packages.
* **Newsletter**: Integrated email marketing for weekly schedule announcements.

## Phase 6: Business Intelligence
* **Analytics Dashboard**: Visual charts for most popular dishes and peak catering months.
* **Inventory Tracking**: Stock management for catering ingredients.
* **Multi-Truck Support**: Ability to manage multiple trucks from a single dashboard.
